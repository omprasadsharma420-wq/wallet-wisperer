# Product Foundation

## Positioning

YNAB is a budgeting methodology for planners. Wallet Whisperer is a close-the-day behavioral ritual for people who will not run a full budget.

## V1 Foundation

- Smart Capture Inbox is the backbone.
- AI creates possible transaction drafts.
- The user confirms at night.
- Confirmed flexible spending is translated against one active Rival goal.
- The daily report is the retention anchor.

## Product Rules

1. Do not compete with YNAB on budgeting completeness.
2. Do not build a "how much can I still spend?" permission bar.
3. Do not shame fixed/needed expenses.
4. Do not make AI output final without user review.
5. Do not make automatic capture sound universal.
6. Keep one active goal in V1.
7. Treat cash as first-class because bank-linked systems miss it.

## V1 Must-Haves

- Auth.
- Goal setup.
- Manual quick log.
- Pasted/email Smart Capture Draft.
- Draft review and confirmation.
- Transaction table.
- Rival percentage for flexible spending.
- Close-day report.
- Streak state.
- Minimal dashboard summary.
- Nightly review count and notification copy.
- Recurring fixed-expense draft generation.

## V1 Could-Haves

- Inbound email webhook.
- Receipt OCR text input.
- Android notification adapter.
- CSV export.

## Explicitly Out

- Investment tracking.
- Bank linking.
- Full email inbox scanning.
- SMS reading.
- Multi-account/household sharing.
- Rich envelope budgeting.
